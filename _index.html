Michel Steuwer

I'm a postdoctoral research associate at the University of Edinburgh in the CArD group. I am a member of the Institute for Computing Systems Architecture (ICSA) which is part of the School of Informatics.

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Münster.

I'm interessted in all aspects of parallel programming. Particular research interests of mine include structured parallel programming, heterogeneous and GPU computing, and novel functional compilation techniques.
